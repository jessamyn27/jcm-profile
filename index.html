about me

Hi, I'm Caleb McTwigan

I am a motion graphics animator, graphic designer, & video/audio editor, producing compelling stories for brands and concepts. With more than 16 years combined experience, I like to utilize a broad set of techniques to find new and innovative ways to create impressive & unique content. I have spent most of my career working in art, or in conjunction with artists. This includes having roles as:
  • Motion Designer
  • Graphic Designer & Illustrator
  • Website Designer & Developer
  • Audio & Video Creation
  • Script & Code Writer
  • Art Workflow Optimization
  • Project Manager
  • Report Developer
Outside of my creative and professional endeavors, you may find me watching a movie or show, reading a good children's book... to my children, attempting to cling on to my dominance over the aforementioned children at video games, or narrowly avoiding electrocuting, maiming, or injuring myself while doing some DIY projects at home!
